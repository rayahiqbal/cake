How to Bake a Cake

Follow these steps to bake a delicious cake!

Step 1: Gather Ingredients
Step 2: Mix Ingredients
Step 3: Preheat Oven
Step 4: Bake
Step 5: Decorate

Step 1: Gather Ingredients

Before you begin, make sure you have all the necessary ingredients such as flour, sugar, eggs, etc.

Step 2: Mix Ingredients

In a large mixing bowl, combine the dry ingredients, then add the wet ingredients and mix until well combined.

Step 3: Preheat Oven

Preheat your oven to the recommended temperature stated in your recipe.

Step 4: Bake

Pour the batter into a greased cake pan and place it in the preheated oven. Bake for the specified time.

Step 5: Decorate

Once the cake has cooled, you can decorate it with frosting, sprinkles, and any other toppings you desire.
